Delete Your Account

Last Updated: December 14, 2025

App: Find Differences - Best Images!
Developer: DC Bros Studio

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time. This page explains how to request account deletion and what data will be removed.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the App: Launch "Find Differences - Best Images!" on your device
  2. Go to Profile: Tap the profile icon in the top-right corner of the home screen
  3. Access Settings: Tap the settings/gear icon on the profile screen
  4. Delete Account: Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm: Read the warning message and confirm that you want to permanently delete your account
Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed from our servers.

Data Deletion Details

When you delete your account, the following data is affected:

Data Type Action Retention Period
User Profile (Display Name, Region) Deleted Immediate
Profile Image / Avatar Deleted Immediate
Game Scores & Rankings Deleted Immediate
Leaderboard Entries Deleted Immediate
Device Identifier (UUID) Deleted Immediate
Google Account Link Unlinked Immediate
Anonymous Analytics (aggregated, non-personal) Retained Indefinite

Third-Party Data

Please note that third-party services used by our app may retain some data according to their own policies:

  • Google AdMob: Ad interaction data is managed by Google. See Google's Privacy Policy to manage your ad preferences.
  • Firebase: Authentication tokens are invalidated upon account deletion.

Alternative: Contact Us

If you are unable to delete your account through the app (e.g., you no longer have access to your device), you can request account deletion by contacting us directly.

To request account deletion via email:

  1. Send an email to dcbros.studio@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include your display name and the email address associated with your Google account (if you signed in with Google)

We will process your request within 7 business days and send you a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.
